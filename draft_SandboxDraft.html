How It Works

Select two elements from the periodic table using the dropdown menus. Click "Mix Elements" to see the result of their chemical reaction. The sandbox will display the chemical formula, compound name, and reaction type.

This educational tool demonstrates basic chemical bonding principles and helps visualize how elements combine to form compounds.

Bond Types

  • Ionic: Electron transfer between metals and nonmetals
  • Covalent: Electron sharing between nonmetals
  • Metallic: Electron sharing between metals
  • Coordinate: Both electrons from one atom

Periodic Trends

Atomic Radius

Increases down groups, decreases across periods

Electronegativity

Increases across periods, decreases down groups

Reactivity

Metals: increases down groups, Nonmetals: decreases down groups
